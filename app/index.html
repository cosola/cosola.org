Complex Systems Optimization is an independent research lab. It foster multidisciplinary collaboration to sustain a learning and productive environment where ideas, creativity, methods, and technologis integrate to explore and design optimal solutions to complex sociotechnical problems.

COSOLA

Complex Systems Optimization engages talented young people from less advantaged communities in the process of learning: asking bold big questions and exploring the frontiers of complexity science.

Research

Socio-Technical Systems

Education and Learning is the are of research we have focused for the last 12 years. How to build a resilient. self-motivating, learning-passionate students who are extremelly smart, but were born into poverty and have been immersed since birth in discouraging environments.

SALVIA-Regional Public Health

Public Health at the interception of data science and artificial intelligence.SALVIA focuses on making health information readily accessible, private, and current. A regional health data system to improve public health.

Human Learning; Machine Learning

Advancing learning and understanding of complex adaptive systems in less advanced communities. COSOLA advances STEM and complex systems engineering education. It advances research and solutions to complex socio-technical problems.

Human Migration Dynamics

Throughout the human history, migration dynamics has been a phenomum motivated by the exploratory spirit, conflicts, and economics. Migration has transformed civilizations and given rise to cultures and power. With the rise of nation states, migration has been controlled by a sense of responsibility and security...Implications for education, health, governance, culture, security.

Education

Nurturing talents. Instilling passion for learning.

The journey began in 2007 in the region of Ytabo, Dominican Republic. Since, understanding the complexity of developing lifelong learner has been our priority. We see to improve the lives of talented young people from less advantaged communities through 21st century education.

Through problem-based research, bold questions are explored to discover optimal and sustainable solutions to complex real-world problems. Education is a critical socio-technical system. Our focus is on best practice; understanding how the students learn; providing chanllenging and stimulating learning environments where they can succeed.
